How to Apply

We seek students who are curious, motivated, and eager to contribute to our vibrant community. Follow our streamlined admission process below.

1

Submit Online Application

Fill out the preliminary application form on this page with accurate parent and student details.

2

Campus Tour & Interview

Once your application is reviewed, our admissions office will schedule a personal campus tour and a brief interview.

3

Assessment

Depending on the grade level, candidates may be required to complete a standardized assessment or submit recent academic records.

4

Enrollment Decision

You will receive a formal decision letter within two weeks of completing the assessment and interview process.
