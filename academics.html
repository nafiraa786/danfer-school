Kindergarten
The Early Years

Our Kindergarten program is designed to spark a lifelong love of learning. We believe that play is the work of a child, and our classrooms reflect this philosophy by blending structured learning with imaginative exploration.

  • Play-based, child-centered curriculum focused on holistic development.
  • Introduction to foundational literacy, numeracy, and scientific inquiry.
  • Creative expression through art, music, and dramatic play.
  • Small class sizes (1:12 ratio) ensuring individualized attention.

Grades 1-5

Elementary School
Building Foundations

In Elementary school, students transition into more focused academic disciplines while continuing to develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills in a highly collaborative environment.

  • Comprehensive core subjects: Language Arts, Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies.
  • Integrated STEM methodology with dedicated makerspace access.
  • Foreign language instruction starting in Grade 1 (Spanish or Mandarin).
  • Character education focusing on empathy, resilience, and global awareness.

Grades 6-12

High School
Preparation for the Future

Our rigorous High School program is tailored to prepare students for the demands of top-tier universities and the rapid changes of the modern workforce, emphasizing leadership and academic excellence.

  • Advanced Placement (AP) and International Baccalaureate (IB) options.
  • Extensive university counseling starting in Grade 9.
  • Capstone project requirements involving community service and research.
  • Over 40 extracurricular clubs, competitive athletics, and arts programs.
